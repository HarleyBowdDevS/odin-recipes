One Pan Lemon Chicken and Asparagus

HOME

Try this one pan lemon chicken and asparagus this spring. Tender, juicy chicken breast and crisp asparagus are paired with a delicious Alfredo-like lemon-cream sauce and it is ready in under 30 minutes.By Kathryn Hendrix, RDN, LD

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 4 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 (140 to 170 gram) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 450 grams asparagus, ends trimmed and cut into 2-inch g
  • 1 lemon
  • 3/4 cup whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teapoons Italian seasoning

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Place one chicken breast on a cutting board. Cover chicken breast with plastic wrap and pound with a meat mallet until about 2 centimeters thick. Repeat with remaining chicken breasts.

Step 3

Season chicken with 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.

Step 4

Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a very-large skillet over medium-high heat.Add chicken and cook until golden brown and an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of a breast reads at least 74 degrees C, 8 to 10 minutes, turning halfway. Remove chicken from the skillet; set aside.

Step 5

Add asparagus to the skillet. Cook over medium-high, until bright green and crisp-tender, stirring occasionally, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove asparagus from the skillet; set aside.

Step 6

Juice half of the lemon (about 22 milliliters juice) and slice the remaining half into wedges.

Step 7

Add lemon juice, whipping cream, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and Italian seasoning to the skillet

Step 8

Simmer over medium-high, scraping the bottom of the skillet with a wooden spoon to release any browned bits, 2 minutes. Season with remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper.

Step 9

Add chicken and asparagus back into the skillet. Simmer until heated through and sauce reaches desired consistency, about 1 minute. Serve with lemon wedges.
